Fast Bitcoin Toiletpaper Facts

Bitcoin Toiletpaper has a 10 minute block time, just like Bitcoin.
Bitcoin Toiletpaper has wallets for Windows, MacOS, and Linux.
You can restore a deleted Bitcoin Toiletpaper wallet with your view key and spend key.

Why Bitcoin Toiletpaper?

1. Fast Transactions

Bitcoin Toiletpaper is creating blocks every 10 minutes, just like Bitcoin.

2. Secure

Bitcoin Toiletpaper is based on the robust Bitcoin codebase.

3. Easy To Use

We have easy to use CLI and GUI wallets for Windows, MacOS. and Linux.

4. Easy To Mine

Bitcoin Toiletpaper can be generated through PoW mining. For a mining pool we recommend ours.

5. Community

The Bitcoin Toiletpaper community is very welcoming to all users and developers. Please join us in our Discord Chat.

6. Support

We are growing a community of developers. You can help by testing software and submitting bug reports.
